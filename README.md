# ☕ Coffee Shop Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing coffee shop sales data to extract meaningful insights related to customer behavior, product performance, and store operations. The analysis is visualized through an interactive dashboard that helps in understanding trends and making data-driven decisions.

The dataset used for this project has been sourced from **Kaggle**.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze overall sales performance
- Understand customer purchasing patterns
- Identify top-selling products
- Evaluate store-wise performance
- Analyze peak order hours and busy days
- Study category-wise contribution to total sales

---

## 📈 Key Insights

### 💰 Sales Performance
- **Total Sales:** $698,812.33  
- **Total Footfall:** 149,116  
- **Average Bill per Person:** 4.69  
- **Average Orders per Person:** 1.44  

---

### ⏰ Order Trends
- Peak order hours observed between **8 AM to 10 AM**
- Significant drop in orders after **6 PM**
- Weekdays like **Friday and Thursday** have higher orders

---

### 🛍️ Category Distribution
- **Coffee** contributes the highest share (~39%)
- Followed by **Tea (~28%)**
- Other categories include Bakery, Drinking Chocolate, Flavours, etc.

---

### 🥤 Product Performance
Top 5 products based on sales:
- Barista Espresso
- Brewed Chai Tea
- Hot Chocolate
- Gourmet Brewed Coffee
- Brewed Black Tea

---

### 🏪 Store Performance
- **Hell’s Kitchen** generated the highest sales
- Followed by **Astoria** and **Lower Manhattan**
- Footfall distribution is relatively balanced across locations

---

### 📦 Order Size Distribution
- Large and Regular orders dominate (~30% each)
- Small orders contribute the least (~9%)
